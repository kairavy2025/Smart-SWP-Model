Model Explanation

This Intelligent SWP Model compares a traditional fixed withdrawal strategy with a smart withdrawal strategy. The smart strategy uses moving average signals, volatility levels, inflation adjustment, Monte Carlo simulation, and K-fold validation.

Static SWP

Withdraws a fixed monthly amount and increases it yearly based on inflation. It does not react to market crashes or high volatility.

Smart SWP

Adjusts withdrawals using market guard logic. When the market is bearish or volatility is high, withdrawals are reduced to protect capital.

Monte Carlo Simulation

Tests thousands of possible future market paths to estimate the probability that the portfolio survives for the selected years.

K-Fold Validation

Splits historical data into five periods and checks whether the smart strategy protects more corpus than the static strategy. It validates the model; the future suggestion section uses the latest signal to recommend withdrawals.
